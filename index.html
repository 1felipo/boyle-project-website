Boyle project

Improves learning

Boyle is an educational project. It seeks to generate didactic materials according to the 21st century.

Project goals

Faster and more efficient solutions

With boyle we incorporate technology to be able to verify traditional techniques and also, we generate a reliable source, to improve the quality of the information with which the students carry out their tasks and essays.

Provide educational and technological tools

We are generating different materials so that all schools can enjoy them. Thus, we will be able to achieve an integration, as well as an enhancement of the learning of the technical institutions of our city.

Application of our knowledge

The professional profile of chemistry students does not have scope in the programming area, but with boyle we show that we can expand our knowledge, to achieve better results, always applying our previous knowledge.

Our solutions

Discord bot

A bot that solves different calculations in the area of ​​exact sciences. The objective is to solve and verify in an agile way different situations that arise in the advanced years of the technical career.

Arduino device

A device that measures different laboratory variables. This helps us to check and streamline work on laboratory experiences.
